Phuze

Tayler Gerou

To truly progress in the world one needs to have a strong grasp on the digital and analog world around us; one without the other will cause people to feel unbalanced. Phuze is an educational initiative that helps students refocus on the world, using mindfulness and photographic practices. Phuze is broken down into two, two week sections and the first week teaches core photography skills with the use of Arch, the AI app. The second two weeks dials in on mindfulness practices with the use of lm photography. Mindfulness is about being present in ones surroundings and making a conscious choice about where to place ones attention. Through repeated practice of being mindful, a person can help reduce depression, anxiety, and ADHD while increasing cognitive thinking, creativity and much more. Phuze is the key element in helping students to rethink, refocus, and think independently so they can change and improve the world around them.
