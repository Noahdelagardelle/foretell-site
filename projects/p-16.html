Aerify

Kelsey Mayry

Aerify is a service company to improve the quality of life indoors. Many of us live, work, and spend most of our time indoors. The company will offer a full design service to incorporate indoor plants into a customers required space after testing the indoor air quality. Benefits of indoor plants can be seen in retail, hospitality, medical facilities, workplace design, and in education. It is time to take plants aesthetic qualities and use them functionally to purify the indoors.
