Flip Flop

Andrew Westphal

Flip Flop is a custom smart snowboard goggles company. Flip Flop is made up of two parts, Flip, the digital assistant that helps the rider navigate through goggle interfaces and Flop, the user customizable lens that allows the user to stand out from all other riders. Flip Flop's main goal is to enhance the user’s ride with various technologies like augmented reality, virtual reality, realtime connectivity, and user customization without taking away the snowboarders experience.  At the end of the day its all about the ride.
