Way Back When

Cree Schuh

Way Back When is a monthly subscription that is meant to reconnect people with the past while using the modern technology we have at our disclosure today. To become a member of Way Back When one has to sign in to create an account by using other applications such as Facebook, Spotify, Instagram and just about anything that requires a user name and password. Then our AI will be able to personalize the user’s profile and determine possible interests. From there the user will get monthly packages of items that will connect to the user, and might expand their knowledge or even bring people together. The best thing about Way Back When is that it is a complete surprise! The AI system is able to find the perfect item that reflects the user’s activities. Way Back When is a great source to discover the past, find new interests, and expand your knowledge of pop culture of the 80s’ and 90s’.
