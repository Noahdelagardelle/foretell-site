Hærk

Keenan Strom

Hærk is a streaming service that functions on the basis of trading computing power for free music. By utilizing Blockchain Technology, a network of users are able to both complete transactions to musicians, and also verify transactions needed to solidify the system. This also allows for the elimination of the middle man, it's simply a direct investment of time to the musican. Another focal point of Hærk is to incentivize streaming, by allowing users to maintain a small portion of their generated digital currency. Artificial Intelligence also serves to identify music wherever you are in the world. So whether you're at a concert, in a small club, or grinding in study hall, you can continue to contribute to the Hærk economy.
