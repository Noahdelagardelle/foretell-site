David Nelson

YEANAY

The YEANAY mobile App is conceptually a unique place to share opinions, thoughts and beliefs. The design goal of the App is to motivate people to have both in-depth and short conversations that are intelligent and interesting.  The App user is encouraged to start a conversation or join one, see who agrees or disagrees with their opinions, and then respond. The user may change their mind about their opinion or change the mind of someone else. The App is designed to be fun and easy to use.
