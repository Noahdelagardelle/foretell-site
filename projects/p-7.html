Land—mark

Noah Delagardelle

Land—mark introduces a whole new way to experience America’s State Parks. Using augmented reality land—mark places an invisible world of information, art, and experience on top of these national treasures. Your phone is the window into this new world. Land—mark stands for sustainability and conservation. By adding to the experience by not damaging a single rock, leaf, animal, or blade of grass. Landmark believes that State Parks are among America’s best ideas and should be taken care of, explored, loved, and most of all experienced.
