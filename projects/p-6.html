Flower & Flour Co

Holly Darton

Flower & Flour Co. is a florist and bakery hybrid with a fun twist that gives the customer a memorable experience. Using artificial intelligence in the form of a personality quiz, Flower & Flour Co. knows exactly what floral arrangement and bakery item to present the recipient with. Not only is the floral and bakery combo specifically chosen for you, the gift becomes even more personalized with packaging customization. Share the quiz to your special recipient through the Flower & Flour Co. website, social media, or with a print out at the F&F shop. Share your personal Flower & Flour Co. combo experience through Instagram, Facebook, and Snapchat with the campaign #MyPersonalFlowerFlour.
