Without

Kayla Smith

Bias is inevitable and the media is full of it which can skew perspectives and beliefs. To make matters worse, many people do not take the time to sift through the “Fake News” to get to the truth. Without is a tool that utilizes VR and AI to do the work for you. It's a convenient way for people to compare and contrast sources, and witness current events for themselves to hopefully draw their own conclusions.
