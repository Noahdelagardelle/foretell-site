Vidose

Katrina Strait

Vidose is an application that utilizes AI photo recognition technology to assist users in improving their nutrition. Users take a photo of their meals, and Vidose’s photo recognition will identify the food and analyze the nutritional information. After capturing their meals, users will be able to see the analytics behind what they’re eating, and they will be able to purchase vitamins within the app to supplement their diet. Vidose will simplify nutrition tracking and will make users feel better than ever.
