Tourbus

Eric Collier

Tourbus is an app that will allow performers and promoters to coordinate the entire agenda of an event all in one virtual platform. This app is designed specifically for the local music scene, and is intended only for performers and promoters. The app has special AI features like the “Band Tracker”, where you can see how far away from the venue other performers are. Stop by on May 4th to learn more.
